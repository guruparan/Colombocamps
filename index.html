Colombo

Camps Community

The Colombo Camps Community is an independent community of software developers, with a common goal of improving the competencies of members of the Sri Lankan IT community. This concept of forming the community came about with the ideas of technical personal of the local IT industry, in an effort to bridge the gaps in terms of knowledge and skills. This initiative is a collective attempt to place Sri Lanka on the global map as a hub for IT knowledge services. This community has hosted various forums in the recent past, right from agile methodologies to iPhone developer sessions.
